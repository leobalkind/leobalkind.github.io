A persistent roadmap document. **4000 new items**, in addition to the 700
already discussed in the conversation. Items are intentionally terse — most
need a design pass before implementation. Higher-numbered items inside a
section tend to be more speculative or content-padded.

> **Reading guide:** Sections are alphabetized for navigation. The conversation
> already covered items 1–700 (mobile, audio, PUG SNAKE, etc.) and shipped ~30
> of them. This document captures the broader vision in case future contributors
> want to keep pushing.

## Realistic execution ranking

If a future contributor wants to prioritize, these have the best impact-per-hour ratio:

1. **Mobile bugs + polish** (anything in EE) — actively played by 50% of users
2. **Performance** (BB) — keeps the experience smooth
3. **Local multiplayer** (#20, #461) — high impact, no backend
4. **More PUGFORT enemies** (G) — content with high gameplay value
5. **Achievements** (T) — long-term retention, all client-side
6. **Daily challenge** (#7) — seeded RNG, all client-side
7. **Skin shop** (#13, N) — earn-and-collect loop
8. **New original mini-games** (M) — broaden hub appeal

Skip these unless you have backend + time:
- Online multiplayer, global leaderboard, cloud save, anti-cheat, replays.
- All "online" or "server" prefixed items in Q.

---

**Word of caution:** scaling to ~50 distinct games + 1000 characters + full
multiplayer is a multi-year project for a team. This document is a wishlist,
not a roadmap. Pick a small subset that fits your actual time and audience.
